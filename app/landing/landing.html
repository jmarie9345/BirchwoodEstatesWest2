About Us

Birchwood Estates West is a premier manufactured home community located in Fort Gratiot, MI. The community which has been locally owned and managed since 2002 offers residents affordable, low maintenance living without compromising quality.

We work with our customers to find them the perfect home that fits their lifestyle. We have relationships with multiple manufacturers, offering numerous options at competitive pricing. From the ordering process to the delivery and set-up, we work closely with our customers to ensure their home purchasing process is great.

Community Features

Quiet

Tucked back behind retail space and surrounded by nature, this community offers a quiet, relaxing environment for residents

Low Maintenance

Management takes care of lawn maintenance, snow removal and garbage so less headaches for residents!

Controlled Fees

Lot fees are set at $295/month and are ONLY adjusted based on cost of living changes

Aesthetic Upgrades

Homes are newer than 2001, have attached garages, block skirting and sit on large lots.

Custom Options

You can custom order your home. Choose your interior and exterior features and which lot it sits on.

Convenient Location

Within minutes of shopping, restaruants and medical facilities
